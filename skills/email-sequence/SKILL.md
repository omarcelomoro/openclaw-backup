# Email Sequence Design

Create email sequences that nurture relationships, drive action, and move people toward conversion.

## Before creating a sequence, understand
1. Sequence type
2. Audience context
3. Primary goal and success definition

## Core principles
- One email, one job
- Value before ask
- Relevance over volume
- Clear next step in every email

## Typical sequence lengths
- Welcome: 3-7 emails
- Lead nurture: 5-10 emails
- Onboarding: 5-10 emails
- Re-engagement: 3-5 emails

## Copy rules
- Short paragraphs
- Mobile-first formatting
- Conversational tone
- One clear CTA per email

## Leevre adaptation notes
- Use for consórcio and seguros nurture flows, follow-up after lead capture, objection handling, reactivation, and post-contemplation referrals.
- Emphasize education, authority, trust, and patrimonial strategy rather than spammy urgency.
- Only implement delivery after email infrastructure is professionally configured.
